14 · Design Principles

The Rules.

Six principles that govern every visual decision. When in doubt, return here.

01
Restraint over excess
One accent colour per composition. Generous whitespace. Remove one element from every design — if it improves, it wasn't needed. Premium means less, not more.
02
Texture creates depth
Matte surfaces, grain overlays, subtle grid lines, and material references (metal, stone, concrete) create the tactile luxury feeling that flat digital design cannot. Always add one texture layer.
03
Typography is branding
Bodoni Moda for display. Barlow for hierarchy. Space Mono for precision data. Never substitute. The typefaces carry more brand identity than the colour palette alone.
04
Transformation, not product
Lead every communication with the outcome, not the specification. "Barefoot Sunday morning" before "35mm pile height." Emotion converts. Specifications reassure.
05
Consistency creates trust
The same brand elements — logo treatment, colour, typography — applied identically across WhatsApp profile, vehicle, business card, social post, and website. Trust is earned through repetition.
06
Speed is a design decision
A 5-second website load or a 2-hour mockup response time destroys more brand equity than a poorly chosen font. Operational speed and visual design are inseparable for a premium brand.
