Brand Philosophy

PrimeTurf transforms outdoor spaces into premium lifestyle environments for modern Pretoria East living.

Every surface we lay is a statement of intent: that the outdoor space deserves the same care and craft as the interior.

Primary Use

Deep Forest is the brand. Use it on vehicles, signage, uniforms, proposal covers, and dark backgrounds. It communicates stability, nature, and permanence.

Luxury Accent

Harvest Gold is the marker of premium quality. Use it for monograms, icon highlights, foil applications, dividers, and any element that signals "this is special." Never overuse — its power is in restraint.

Digital Energy

Vivid Green appears sparingly — CTAs, notification badges, WhatsApp accents, hover states. It is the heartbeat of the brand in motion. Never use on print luxury materials.

Voice in Practice
Not This

"We install fake grass in Pretoria. Get a free quote. Affordable prices for your lawn."

This

"Your outdoor space is not an afterthought — it is an extension of how you choose to live. PrimeTurf transforms it accordingly."

Tone: Calm Authority

Never loud. Never desperate. Speak like a company that does not need to convince — because the work speaks for itself.

Register: Aspirational

Speak to who the client wants to be. Their best self lives in an estate with a perfectly presented outdoor space.

Pace: Measured

Short sentences. White space between thoughts. Luxury does not rush. Every word earns its place.

11

Brand Applications

Every touchpoint is a brand expression. From the first WhatsApp message to the vehicle in the driveway — the standard never drops.

Vehicle Wraps
Matte black fleet. Gold PT monogram. Full name. Contact in Montserrat 300. No installation photos. Estate-level first impression on every road.
Uniforms
Deep Forest polo with embroidered PT gold monogram. Charcoal chinos. No visible branding below the waist. Polished, not tradesman.
Signage
Brushed steel or dark powder-coat. Gold-routed letterforms. Cinzel 600. At scale: monogram only. Always mounted, never adhesive vinyl.
Business Cards
Black card. Gold foil PT monogram front. Minimal contact reverse on warm white. Thick stock. Matte finish. The card that gets kept.
Proposal Covers
Dark linen-texture cover with embossed PT mark. Client name in Cormorant italic. Gold corner accents. Inside pages on ivory. Watermarked with PT monogram at 8%.
WhatsApp Branding
Profile: PT gold monogram on forest. Business name: PrimeTurf. Auto-message: measured, professional. No emoji. No "Hi!! How can I help." Calm authority from the first message.
Social Templates
Instagram: dark frame, gold divider, Cinzel caption. Stories: full-bleed estate photo, minimal gold PT tag. No loud typography. The feed looks like a portfolio, not a hardware store.
Stationery Suite
Letterhead on 120gsm ivory. PT mark top-right, gold horizontal rule. Footer: address in Montserrat 300, 9pt. Envelopes: forest green with gold wax seal stamp.
Estate Signage
On-site installation boards: dark face, gold Cinzel name, Montserrat contact. No photos. No cluttered info. A sign that belongs in the driveway of the home it's branding.
