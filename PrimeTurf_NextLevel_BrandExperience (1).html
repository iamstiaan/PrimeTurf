Why naming matters
"40mm Deluxe Grass" is a product spec.
"PrimeTurf Estate" is an aspiration.

Names carry perceived value. A client buying "Estate" feels different to a client buying "Grade B Premium Artificial Grass." One belongs in a quote. One belongs on a plaque.
The naming architecture
Every range name signals a lifestyle context:
Signature — quality as standard
Riviera — relaxed luxury, water
Estate — architecture, prestige
Horizon — scale, vision, modernity
EliteSport — performance, precision
