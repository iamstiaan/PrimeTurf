09 · Quoting & Installation Standards
The Craft of Execution
Proposal Standard
  • Branded cover page — client name, address, date
  • Executive summary — what they asked for and what we recommend
  • Product specification with photos and warranty
  • Two options (Option A / Option B) — always
  • Investment summary — clean, clear, no confusion
  • Process timeline — when, how long, what to expect
  • Warranty and aftercare commitment
  • Acceptance and deposit instructions
Installation Non-Negotiables
  • All team in uniform before entering any site
  • Access points protected — no marks on paving
  • Site cleaner on departure than on arrival
  • Base depth checked before turf is laid
  • Grain direction consistent throughout
  • All seams pressure-tested before sign-off
  • Stefan approves before client walkthrough begins
  • Photo documentation: 4 angles + 2 detail shots minimum

"Would we be proud for this to appear on our Instagram? If the answer is anything other than yes — it is not finished."

11 · Referral System

16 · Aligned Incentives
Shared Growth Philosophy

PrimeTurf is being built around a shared-growth philosophy. Contribution, execution, responsibility, and results create opportunity. As the business grows, the people helping build it participate in that growth.

Founding Principle · Leon van Eeden
The Four Pillars
01 · Performance Contribution
Each role has defined outcomes. When those outcomes are consistently exceeded — the reward reflects that contribution. Quality, speed, conversion, and client satisfaction are measurable.
02 · Growth Participation
As PrimeTurf grows through territory expansion, increased volume, or new revenue streams — the team that enabled that growth participates in the rewards. Growth is not a bonus to the founders alone.
03 · Sustainability First
The business must remain financially healthy before individual upside is realised. A stable, growing company creates more long-term value for everyone than one that peaks and contracts.
04 · Future Opportunity
The founding team has first participation rights in future verticals, expansion opportunities, and any structural growth events. Building PrimeTurf from the start is the highest-value position available.
Compensation Principles
  • Structures remain flexible, scalable, and performance-linked
  • Reviewed quarterly — open conversation, no surprises
  • Financial transparency: key metrics shared monthly
  • Milestone triggers: business milestones unlock upside reviews
  • Any team member can raise questions at any quarterly review
Future Participation May Include
  • Performance incentives
  • Growth bonuses
  • Leadership opportunities
  • Expansion participation
  • Strategic partnerships

Everyone who helps build PrimeTurf should benefit from PrimeTurf's success.
