Executive Summary

Founders · Confidential

PrimeTurf is a premium outdoor transformation company targeting Pretoria East's high-income residential and commercial segment. This blueprint is the operating system for building, scaling, and eventually franchising the PrimeTurf model.

The Opportunity
Underserved Premium Segment
Pretoria East hosts some of South Africa's wealthiest residential estates, yet lacks a consistently premium outdoor transformation brand. Most incumbents compete on price. PrimeTurf competes on experience and standard.
The Model
Systems Before Scale
Dominate a defined geographic territory first. Install the full operational infrastructure — sales, delivery, digital, referral — before expanding. Build once. Deploy repeatedly.
The Advantage
Brand as Moat
A premium brand built from the first day creates a compounding competitive barrier. Clients in estates talk. A recognised PrimeTurf vehicle in the driveway does more marketing than any paid ad.

Sales Philosophy
Consultation Not Pitch
PrimeTurf sales operate on the principle of expert consultation. The salesperson's role is to deeply understand the client's vision, advise on the best solution, and present the investment with confidence. No high-pressure tactics. No discounting to win. Clients at this level respond to expertise and trust.
Closing Tactics
Premium Signals
Reference similar premium installations nearby. Use social proof with before/after examples. Emphasise warranty, quality of materials, and installation process. Offer a clear next step. The goal is the client feeling confident in their decision, not coerced into it. A client who chooses PrimeTurf freely becomes an advocate.

Referral System

In Pretoria East's estate communities, a referral carries more weight than any advertisement. The referral system is PrimeTurf's most powerful growth channel.

Referral Trigger
Post-Install Invitation
The referral ask is embedded naturally in the 7-day follow-up message. It is not a transaction — it is an invitation. "If you know someone who would love a result like this, we'd love an introduction."
Referral Incentive
Meaningful Reward
Referring clients receive a meaningful reward — service credit, complimentary maintenance visit, or premium gift. The value matches the calibre of the client. Not a voucher. Something considered.
Estate Strategy
Neighbour Campaigns
After completing an installation in a complex or estate, a tasteful introduction is made to the estate management. Physical estate-facing signage drives organic neighbour enquiries for 4–6 weeks post-install.
Referral Tracking

All referrals are tracked in the CRM with a referral source field. Every new client is asked "How did you hear about PrimeTurf?" Referral rates are reviewed monthly. Stiaan tracks the digital referral sources (social shares, Google reviews leading to calls). Mechanne tracks direct referrals.

Before & After Campaign System

Capture Protocol
Mandatory On Every Job
Before photo: same angle, good light, wide enough to show context. After photo: identical angle and distance. Golden hour where possible. Stefan is responsible for capture. Client permission confirmed at booking stage.
Editing Standard
Consistent Treatment
Consistent Lightroom preset or template. Deep forest and gold tone. Not over-filtered. The before must show the problem. The after must show the transformation. Both must be honest and compelling.
Distribution
Multi-Channel Deploy
Instagram post + reel reveal. Facebook post. Google Business photo upload. Website gallery (ongoing). Project blog feature for large installs. Client's before/after is their referral tool — share directly with them too.
The Before/After Asset Is PrimeTurf's Most Valuable Marketing Collateral

A single compelling before/after from a recognisable Pretoria East estate carries more conversion power than any paid ad. It proves capability, shows area relevance, and triggers the "I want that" response in the target audience. Building a library of 50+ high-quality before/afters is a primary Year 1 asset goal.

Aligned Incentives & Shared Upside

"As PrimeTurf grows, the people helping build it should grow with it. Value is created together. Reward must follow contribution."
PrimeTurf · Founding Compensation Principle

PrimeTurf operates on an aligned incentives model during the founding stage. Rather than fixed commissions that create rigid cost structures, the framework is built on shared upside — everyone grows as the company grows. Specific structures are agreed between founders based on contribution, role, and the company's financial reality at each stage.

Founding Stage
Build Mode
Contribution is recognised through equity-equivalent arrangements, deferred reward, or agreed draw structures based on company revenue
All founders sacrifice short-term for long-term upside
Every rand reinvested in the system creates compound future value
Non-financial benefits: ownership of domain, career equity, future opportunity
Growth Stage
Earn Mode
As revenue stabilises, agreed draw structures formalised per founder role and contribution level
Performance milestones unlock increased participation in company value
Growth achievements create defined bonus events tied to territory wins, volume thresholds, and brand milestones
Reviewed quarterly. Adjusted as company finances allow.
Scale Stage
Compound Mode
Territory expansion creates new participation opportunities for founding team members
Franchise or licensing model creates asset-value events beyond operational income
Founders who built the system own a proportionate piece of what the system becomes
Value at exit or partial sale distributed per founding agreements
Incentive Design Principles
Performance

Reward is proportional to measurable contribution and outcomes, not just effort or time.

Sustainability

No structure should compromise the company's ability to reinvest in growth or weather a slow period.

Transparency

All founders have visibility into P&L. No information asymmetry. Trust is the foundation of every arrangement.

Future Opportunity

The greatest incentive is the opportunity to own and lead a nationally recognised brand. This document is the map to that outcome.
